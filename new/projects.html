Open Graphing Calculator
Website: www.opengraphingcalculator.com Pictures:

Open Graphing Calculator began as an idea for a research project for College of the Sequoias' Student Undergraduate Research Group Experience as my Professor John Redden and I worked on it for the research project, it grew big enough that we branched it off into it's own project. We worked with JavaScript, jQuery, JSXGraph, MathQuill, and BootStrap.
