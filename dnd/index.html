In the beginning there was once only three nations: The Humans of Vangard, ruled by King Fredrick the Brave. The Dwarfs of Azyria, ruled by Azur-Nasipal the Ruthless. And the Elfs of Catlan, ruled by King Pelagius the Cunning. Warring against one another, the fiendish Orcs found their time to strike and caused an uproar around the middle of Catlan. This gave way for Azur-Nasipal to have the upper hand and attack Catlan. Fredrick, however, aided King Pelagius. For he believed that the Orcs were much too savage to be civilized.

Alas King Fredrick lost his life trying to win a hopeless war. The orcs won and took over a third of Catlan, what is now called Iberia, and split Catlan in half. It wasn't until his great grandson, young Prince Fredrick IV, took the throne and stopped all this bloodshed. He established a nation where people from everywhere can live freely, trade freely, and find opportunities to make a better life. The King has become old now, and by that time he has successfully attained the peace he wanted.


You guys as the players will start in a small town called Four Creeks. You guys own a house there and are roomates. Yet the both of you have no money left and must scavange work to pay for the rent and food. I will be using this site to document all the people and places you guys have explored and learned about. I will also create a journal about your guy's journey.
