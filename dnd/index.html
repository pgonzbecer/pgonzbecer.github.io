Vangard


Click to Enlarge

In the tales of old, there was once only three nations: The Humans of Vangard. The Dwarfs of Azyria. And the Elfs of Catlan. Now there stand five, due to the Orcish uprising, which split Catlan in half. The great King of Vangard helped Catlan against this uprising, cementing their allegiance to one another. Though the king fought hard, the war was lost and with it, his life. Generations have past and although Vangard still has some hatred against Iberia, the Orcish Nation, that will not stop them on achieving world peace.


You guys as the players will start in a small town called Four Creeks. You guys own a house there and are roomates. Yet the both of you have no money left and must scavange work to pay for the rent and food. I will be using this site to document all the people and places you guys have explored and learned about. I will also create a journal about your journey.

Four Creeks

The following is stuff you should know about this small town:

Michael, the Barkeep

Michael is just a simple barkeep. Though he never really expresses emotion and talks with such a monotone voice, he aids everyone with finding work. Such as clearing out a cave filled with goblins or something.


Kevin and Keith, the Valice Brothers

Kevin is an arrogant Fighter who loves to compete against others. He passes his time by working at the Valice Inn with his father, working on odd jobs that the pub puts up every once in a while, or by getting violently drunk. Keith, the younger brother, is a Bard who plays for the drunks over at the pub. He is more of a diplomat than his brother, though he will always go on odd jobs with his bigger brother to make sure he is ok.


The Clerics of Sarenrae, Lilliana and Jerald

These clerics are located at the church, they are healing clerics so they will heal whomever asks. Lilliana is the mother to Kevin and Keith, she has asked you before to take care of her children if you ever see them on one of your quests. Jerald on the other hand was once evil, he has then put that aside and redeemed himself as a healer. Although you still don't know who he used to work for or what exactly he did.
